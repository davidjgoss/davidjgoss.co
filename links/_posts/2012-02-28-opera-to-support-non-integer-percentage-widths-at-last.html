--- title: Opera to support non-integer percentage widths (at last) meta: linked_url: http://my.opera.com/desktopteam/blog/2012/02/28/precision-engine ---

Why it took so long will always be a mystery to me, but here we are: Opera now supports percentage widths correctly, so if you're using fluid grids with percentage widths at several decimal places, Opera will stop ruining your layout by rounding them up.

(via @stephband)
